Introduction

Welcome to your personal medical assistant chatbot. Here to provide reliable health information, symptom checking, and support to help you manage your health better.

🛑 Note: The chatbot provides informational support only and does not replace professional medical advice. Always consult a healthcare professional for urgent or complex conditions.

Medical Features

  • 💊 Provide information about medications and dosages
  • 🩺 Help check symptoms and suggest possible conditions
  • 📅 Schedule or remind you of medical appointments
  • 🍎 Offer health tips and personalized wellness advice
  • 📞 Guide you to emergency services if urgent care is needed
  • 🔒 Keep all your health data secure and confidential

Example Prompts You Can Try

Try asking the chatbot questions like:

  • ❓ What are the side effects of ibuprofen?
  • 💡 Should I see a doctor for my persistent headache?
  • 🩺 I have a fever and cough, what could this mean?
  • 📅 Remind me about my upcoming doctor appointment
  • 🍎 How can I improve my sleep quality?

🧪 Try This - Interactive Examples

How to Use the Medical Chatbot

  1. Open the chat window on the bottom right corner of your screen.
  2. Type your medical question or concern in the text box.
  3. Press Enter or tap Send to receive helpful information or guidance.
  4. Follow the recommendations or contact your healthcare provider if necessary.

Common Questions (FAQ)

What if the chatbot doesn’t respond?

Refresh your browser or check your internet connection. If the issue persists, contact support.

How do I report inaccuracies or issues with medical info?

Use the contact form below or email our support team with details.

Troubleshooting

If the chatbot isn’t responding or seems slow, try the following:

  • Reload the webpage
  • Check your internet connection
  • Clear your browser cache
  • Use a different browser or device
